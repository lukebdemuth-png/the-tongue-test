# Style Guide

Brand direction:

- warm
- minimal
- cinematic
- elegant
- non-distracting

Colors:

- orange glow: `#CF6F1A`
- warm white text: `#F6F0E8`
- soft charcoal plate: `#181512`

Typography:

- primary: `Gotham Medium`
- fallback: `Helvetica Neue` or `Arial`

Sizes for Reels:

- body area label: `26-34`
- main cue text: `38-52`
- CTA text: `58-76`

Safe placement:

- keep captions above the lower caption/UI region
- keep cue boxes away from extreme screen edges
- keep body dots directly over the anatomical point
