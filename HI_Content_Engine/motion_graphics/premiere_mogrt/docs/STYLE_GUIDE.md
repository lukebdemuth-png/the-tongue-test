# Style Guide

Format:

- `1080 x 1920`
- Vertical Instagram Reels

Brand colors:

- Primary orange: `#CF6F1A`
- Warm white: `#F6F0E8`

Fonts:

- Primary: `Gotham-Medium`
- Secondary: `Gotham-Book`

Readability guidance:

- Hook / CTA main line: about `108-118`
- Quote center: about `86`
- Lower-third main line: about `50-52`
- Lower-third accent line: about `28-30`
- Body highlight label: about `34-46`
- Body highlight centered tag: about `104`
- Key-point number: about `92`
- Key-point headline: about `48`
- Key-point detail: about `36`

Safe-area guidance:

- Keep important text away from the bottom caption area.
- Keep the speaker lower-third and quote lower-third above the lower UI.
- Keep the hook and CTA in the center safe region.

Timing suggestions:

- Hook: `1.0s` to `2.0s`
- Quote overlay: `2.0s` to `4.0s`
- Each key point: `1.2s` to `2.0s`
- Speaker ID: `2.0s` to `4.0s`
- CTA: `1.5s` to `2.5s`
