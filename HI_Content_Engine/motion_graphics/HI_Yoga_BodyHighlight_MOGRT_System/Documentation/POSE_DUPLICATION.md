# How To Duplicate For Other Poses

1. Duplicate your reel sequence in Premiere.
2. Keep the MOGRT stack order the same.
3. Reposition the glow point, glow sweep, and callout line over the new body area.
4. Replace the cue text with the new instruction.
5. Shorten or extend each MOGRT clip on the timeline as needed.

Suggested body areas:

- hands / palms
- wrists
- shoulders
- spine
- hips
- hamstrings
- knees
- calves
- heels
- neck / jaw
